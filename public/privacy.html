UniBreeze Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

UniBreeze ("we", "the app") helps high-school students organize their college-application process — tracking schools, deadlines, and tasks, and importing documents the student chooses.

Information we collect

When you create an account we store your email address and a securely hashed password. As you use the app we store the schools, tasks, deadlines, and documents you add. If you connect Canvas or Google Drive, we store only what is needed to show the items you choose.

Google Drive access

UniBreeze uses Google's "Drive file" permission (drive.file), which lets the app access only the specific files you select in the Google Picker. We never request access to your full Drive. Selected files are downloaded once so they can be added to your document vault and turned into a task. UniBreeze's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

How we use your information

Your data is used solely to provide the app's features to you. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising. AI features process your content only to generate the help you request.

Data retention and deletion

Your data remains until you delete it or your account. You can remove schools, tasks, and documents at any time, and disconnect Canvas or Google Drive whenever you like.
